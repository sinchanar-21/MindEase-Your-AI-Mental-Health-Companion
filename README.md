
# MindEase - Your AI Mental Health Companion

Accessible, stigma-free wellness support powered by AI

---

## 🚀 Overview
MindEase is a **privacy-first AI web app** designed to make mental wellness support accessible, engaging, and stigma-free. It empowers students and professionals to build emotional resilience through journaling, coping strategies, peer support, and gamification.

---

## 🌍 The Problem
- **1 in 5 adults** experience a mental health condition each year, with most never receiving care.
- **75% of students** report significant stress or burnout affecting academic performance.
- Therapy costs ($150–$300/session) and stigma create barriers to care.

MindEase addresses these challenges by providing **always-available, anonymous, and affordable support**.

---

## ✨ Features
- **Mood Journaling** – Daily entries with AI-powered sentiment analysis.
- **Coping Strategies** – Personalized mindfulness, breathing, and gratitude prompts.
- **Peer Support** – Anonymous forums for safe, supportive community connection.
- **Gamification** – Streaks and badges to build consistent wellness habits.
- **Privacy-First Design** – End-to-end encryption, anonymous-by-default.

---

## 🛠 Tech Stack
- **Frontend:** React.js (deployed on Vercel/Netlify)
- **Backend:** Node.js + Express (Render/Heroku)
- **Database:** MongoDB (flexible schema-free storage)
- **AI/ML:** Python (Flask/FastAPI) + HuggingFace Transformers for NLP
- **Deployment:** Vercel, Render, AWS, Google Cloud

---

## 📊 Demo Flow
1. **Login** – Secure, anonymous onboarding  
2. **Mood Input** – Quick daily journal entry  
3. **Sentiment Analysis** – NLP model interprets emotional tone  
4. **Suggestions** – Tailored coping exercises delivered  
5. **Dashboard** – Mood trends and growth insights
